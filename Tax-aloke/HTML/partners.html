OUR Partners

Audit Assurance Partners with both EROs and Tax Professional to relief them of any headache of dealing with there clients IRS audit, and tax burdens during the off season. Tax audits can be complex and may require a significant about of time routine communication with the IRS. Audit Assurance relieves tax professionals from the arduous and time consuming task of analyzing IRS correspondence and then re-analyzing clients’ records and documents. All cases are handled in a prompt, courteous and professional manner.

Unique Coverage

Audit Assurance reaches out and goes the extra mile in ways other audit assistance plans do not. When credits, such as EIC, Child Tax Credit and Education Credit are denied, Audit assurance will assist clients even if the return is yet unfunded by the IRS. In addition, the Protection Plus Audit Assistance plan includes Schedule A, Schedule C and Schedule E for example, at no additional charge.

Tax Preparation Guarantee

The taxpayer can rest easy knowing participating tax preparers’ services are guaranteed. If a legitimate preparer error is made during the filing of a tax return, the taxpayer will be reimbursed for the consequent amounts due to the IRS up to the first $2500*.

Tax Debt Relief

Audit Assurance now offers help to taxpayers who owe debts to the IRS. When additional taxes, penalties, and interest are assessed, working with the IRS can be a confusing and stressful experience. Our staff will work with the taxpayer for a resolution to their debt. Tax Debt
